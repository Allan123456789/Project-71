St.Francis Xavier School

Hi!

St.Francis Xavier School is recognized as a full fledged secondary school by the Brihan Mumbai Municipal Corporation and by the education department.

It is situated in Kanjur village. It started as a primary school in 1969 by Late Rev. Fr.Leslie Miranda and graduated to a high school in 1980. The school stands for excellence, development of skills and character formation . Though attention is given to the moral and spiritual formation of all, besides academic, social and physical development. The schools motto is "Wisdom is Life".
